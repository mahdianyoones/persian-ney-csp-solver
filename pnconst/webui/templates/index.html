Persian Ney Construction Aid!

Welcome to my project!

To construct a Persian Ney, you need to have a few reed pieces at hand and make some measurements.

When you go to a canebroker (a place where canes grows in bunch like a garden), you need to cut a few cane branches from the most bottom parts; as shown in the pictures below.

You can also purchase canes from locals, which usually advertise on Instagram.

Then you need to put them in a dark and dry place for a few months to get drained.

Thenafter, you need to cut each cane branch at their junctions, resulting in several chuncks which in this project are called reed pieces.

Using a culis and a simple ruler, you need to make some measurements for each reed piece.

  • The length of the reed in milimeters.
  • The diamter of the reed in milimeters.
  • The thickness of the reed in milimeters.
  • The roundness of the reed in milimeters.

Also, you need to label each reed piece so that later you can distinguish them.
You can simply label them with integer numbers 1, 2, 3, 4, etc and attach this label to the piece.

An example is shown below.

Now you let the AI to handle the rest!

Add reed pieces to the database below one by one.
Then choose your desired Ney register from the settings.
Finally click find solutions in the design section below to get optimal ways to construct your desired Ney!
Note that the algorithm tries to find matches for your desired so that the final instruments have perfect frequency and certain chriteria.
Also, if you choose more than one register, the algorithm finds the matches for all of them so that you can construct a complete set of Neys with your reed pieces.
The latter option is where the power of AI outperforms that of humans in design.
